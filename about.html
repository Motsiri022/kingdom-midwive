About Kingdom Midwives

Empowering women to embrace their God-given calling and live a life of significance

Who We Are

We are a faith-based, non-profit organization aimed to empower and support Kingdom women and their communities to achieve their optimal God-given potential. The Kingdom Midwives employs biblical tools and principles to help Kingdom people to reach their goals and successively live a life of significance. We are an organization that recognizes, transforms and deploys change catalysts to positively influence families, communities and nations with their unique gifts and talents.

Our Mandate

As part of fulfilling the Great Commission, The Kingdom Midwives aims be a leading organization committed to bringing hope and promoting stewardship to women by encouraging them to embrace their unique God-given calling in living a surrendered life unto our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.

Our Programs

Comprehensive support for every woman's journey

1

Events

These life changing events are a great platform for women to connect and engage in transformational conversations. We have relevant topics with phenomenal speakers that are professional in their fields. Women get an opportunity to network and form collaborations, friendships, partnerships and most importantly; sisterhood.

2

Leadership Development

We have dedicated women who have volunteered to offer mentorship and support to women in leadership to achieve their goals and be the best in their fields using our unique leadership development strategies. Our 2030 leadership vision is to touch the lives of 1 million women leaders by 2030.

3

Community Outreach & Disaster Relief

The aim of our community outreach & disaster relief programs is to restore and revive those who have been stigmatized, marginalized and crushed in spirit. Our empowering and uplifting interventions will leave women and their families with sense of worth and renewed hope.

"For I was hungry and you gave me food, I was thirsty and you gave me drink, I was a stranger and you welcomed me, I was naked and you clothed me, I was sick and you visited me, I was in prison and you came to me." ~ Matthew 25:35-36

4

The Kingdom Retreats

We have annual weekend retreats, where women escape the business of the city and the demand of corporate to recuperate, relax and plan. Connect and find themselves.

5

Full Circle Healing Sessions

Our group therapy sessions are very intimate to create a safe space and to allow women to share and offload their burdens without fear of being victimized or judged.

6

Prayer Meetings

We have witnessed that you can never separate a woman from her God. We have scriptural references of women seeking refuge from God. We have prayer meetings for spiritual edification and fellowship.

Our Story

The Kingdom Midwives was originally founded in 2015 under the name Pull-Her-Up Women Empowerment Movement. Pull Her Up succeeded in building relationships and connections among women through Retreats, High-teas, Seminars and Healing sessions which inspired and helped many women to grow in the knowledge of who and whose they are. To-date, The Kingdom Midwives has many members, prayer volunteers, prayer intercessors, and partners and aspire to expand globally.
